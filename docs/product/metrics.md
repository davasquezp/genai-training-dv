# Metrics (lean)

## North-star (early)
- **Interest registrations per week**

## Funnel (minimum viable analytics)
1) Landing page views
2) Interest page views
3) Submit attempts
4) Submit successes
5) Submit failures (with reason buckets)

## Quality metrics
- % of submissions versus at least one interaction with inputs on interest page
- Role distribution (lead/follower)

## Community metrics
- Communities created per week
- % of communities active versus created 
- Tier-limit reached rate (how often communities hit the configured cap)

## Monetization metrics (early)
- Community tier upgrades (count and conversion rate)
- Ticketing volume (gross ticket sales processed)
- Commission revenue (net)

## Operational metrics (supporting)
- Submit success rate
- Error rate (4xx/5xx) on interest submissions
