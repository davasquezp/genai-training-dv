# Product vision (LatinVibe)

## Problem
Latin dance communities are fragmented across chats, spreadsheets, and social media. It’s hard to:
- discover nearby dancers by role/style
- understand what the community wants to learn/do next
- coordinate events/classes in a lightweight way

## Target users
- **Dancers**: leads/followers looking for practice partners and events
- **Community builders**: organizers, instructors, ambassadors

## Value proposition
LatinVibe helps a community **capture interest** and quickly turn it into:
- searchable community insights (who/where/styles)
- lightweight matching/coordination (later phases)
- monetization opportunities for communities (tiers, paid offerings)

## Product principles
- Ship simple first (interest registration + insights)
- Respect privacy (minimal data, clear consent)
- Mobile-first UX (fast forms, low friction)
